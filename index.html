ABOUT ME
Introduction

Hello friends, my name is Geordy Paul Williams. I'm a junior studying software engineering at Kansas State University. Although I was born in southern California, my hometown is Overland Park, Kansas. My interests include video games, movies, music, podcasts and pretty much anything tech related. Feel free to check out my blog to read more about what interests me.

What I'm up to These Days

In my junior year at K-State, I'm taking a really awesome class called Software Architecture and Design (CIS 501) where we get to learn about things like design patterns and principles. I'm also maintaining the website for K-State’s chapter of NSBE (National Society of Black Engineers) which can be found here. Another project I’m working on is building a user interface for dam analysis software as part of a research project with K-State’s Department of Computing and Information Sciences and Sandia National Labs. Lastly, in my spare time, I'm hoping to make time for some android development. I have an idea for an app that gives users information about video games using the api available through Giant Bomb.

Mission Statement

My goal in life is to leave a positive, lasting impact on the world. I will use my strengths and talents responsibly to serve those in need. Knowing that I don't need to see the whole staircase to take the next step, I will step through life as a man of faith, integrity and altruism.

ABOUT ME

Introduction

Hello friends, my name is Geordy Paul Williams. I'm a junior studying software engineering at Kansas State University. Although I was born in southern California, my hometown is Overland Park, Kansas. My interests include video games, movies, music, podcasts and pretty much anything tech related. Feel free to check out my blog to read more about what interests me.

What I'm up to These Days

In my junior year at K-State, I'm taking a really awesome class called Software Architecture and Design (CIS 501) where we get to learn about things like design patterns and principles. I'm also maintaining the website for K-State’s chapter of NSBE (National Society of Black Engineers) which can be found here. Another project I’m working on is building a user interface for dam analysis software as part of a research project with K-State’s Department of Computing and Information Sciences and Sandia National Labs. Lastly, in my spare time, I'm enrolled in Udacity's Full Stack Web Developer Nanodegree. I've been interested in gaining depth in some area of computer science and I couldn't be more excited to take part in this course where I'll get to learn how to build complex web applications.
